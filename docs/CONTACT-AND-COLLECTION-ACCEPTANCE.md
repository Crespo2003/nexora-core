# Contact and collection acceptance

1. Upload a real tenancy agreement in an authenticated workspace.
2. Confirm the tenant and landlord values are extracted and review any low-confidence data.
3. Confirm each tenant and landlord witness is kept as a witness unless the agreement identifies an agent, negotiator, REN number, or representative role.
4. Confirm agents and REN numbers only appear where supported by document evidence.
5. Correct an incorrect role and mark it as a manual correction.
6. Save the tenancy and confirm the linked contact records are scoped to the active workspace.
7. Open the tenant contact, then the landlord contact, and verify their linked tenancy/property details.
8. Use the displayed phone to open WhatsApp; confirm the target is a tenant, authorized representative, agent, or guarantor, never an unauthorised witness.
9. Set a collection follow-up and record a payment promise.
10. Refresh the browser and verify the tenancy, contact role, and follow-up remain present.
11. Upload the same document again and confirm existing extraction reuse without duplicate records.
12. Sign into a second workspace and confirm no contacts, follow-ups, documents, or duplicate-decision history from the first workspace are visible.
